So... what's Seeko Studios?

Seeko Studios was born in Argentina where a small team of artist and developers found each other in a game jam made here! For now, we have prototypes of games made in those jams, but we dream big, and hope that one day we can release a full game, with story and a lot of fun. You can enjoy all of those mini games on our itch.io!

The fact of having a broad vision regarding various instruments despite not standing out in any of them gave me a special place as a musician, I learned to recognize the instrmentation of my favourite games without even noticing.
